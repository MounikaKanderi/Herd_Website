About us


"The Herd," where words flow fearlessly and unapologetically. The name
speaks volumes about what the blog really intends. The herd is not here to
follow the crowd, nor adhere to the status quo. Instead, it stands out
boldly, refusing to be herded into the same pen as everyone else. At The
Herd we connect both readers and writers who are ready to break
barriers in their minds uncovering the deepest truths of many things
happening in and around them. We speak out the untold, show the
unseen and share the necessary.
